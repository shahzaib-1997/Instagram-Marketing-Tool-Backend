What is MassNova

MassNova is a Social Media marketing solution for automated Organic Growth on Instagram.

How does MassNova work

By automating your account on Instagram (primarily follow and unfollow interactions), we make sure that you're getting followbacks only from real profiles that will actually engage with your content.

Once the Instagram account is connected to the MassNova growth system, there are a couple of steps that need to be done in order to see results:

  1. Target other Instagram accounts or hashtags
  2. Wait for a 2-5 day warmup period to end
  3. After the warmup, your account will start doing interactions with other profiles
  4. Keep an eye on your analytics and optimize your targeting for even more growth.

For more information about our growth system please refer to our How it Works, Pricing, and Features pages.
